前言

有必要梳理一下矩阵微分的一些知识

规范定义

2种方式表达微分：

  • Jacobian 矩阵
  • 梯度矩阵

明确两种函数（偏导的分子）：

  • 实值标量函数： f(X)f(\mathbf{X})f(X)
  • 实值矩阵函数： F(X)k=1,l=1p,q=(f11f12⋯f1qf21f22⋯f2q⋮⋮⋱⋮fp1fp2⋯fpq)F(\mathbf{X})_{k=1,l=1}^{p,q}=\begin{pmatrix} f_{11} & f_{12} & \cdots & f_{1q} \\ f_{21} & f_{22} & \cdots & f_{2q} \\ \vdots & \vdots & \ddots & \vdots \\ f_{p1} & f_{p2} & \cdots & f_{pq} \end{pmatrix}F(X)k=1,l=1p,q​=⎝⎜⎜⎜⎛​f11​f21​⋮fp1​​f12​f22​⋮fp2​​⋯⋯⋱⋯​f1q​f2q​⋮fpq​​⎠⎟⎟⎟⎞​

偏导的分母：

  • 向量
  • 矩阵

Jacobian 矩阵

首先是关于向量的微分：

  • 一般而言习惯于分母设置为行向量（行向量偏导算子）：

Dx=def∂∂x=[∂∂x1,∂∂x2,⋯ ,∂∂xm]D_{\text{x}}\overset{\underset{\mathrm{def}}{}}{=}\frac{\partial }{\partial \text{x}}= \left[ \frac{\partial }{\partial x_1}, \frac{\partial }{\partial x_2}, \cdots , \frac{\partial }{\partial x_{m}} \right] Dx​=def​∂x∂​=[∂x1​∂​,∂x2​∂​,⋯,∂xm​∂​]

其次是对于矩阵的微分，有2种定义方式：

  • 向量化:

DX=∂f∂vecT(X)=[∂∂X11,⋯ ,∂∂Xm1,⋯ ,∂∂X1n,⋯ ,∂∂Xmn]D_{\mathbf{X}}=\frac{\partial f}{\partial vec^{T}(\mathbf{X})}=\left[ \frac{\partial }{\partial X_{11}}, \cdots ,\frac{\partial }{\partial X_{m 1}},\cdots , \frac{\partial }{\partial X_{1n}},\cdots , \frac{\partial }{\partial X_{mn}} \right] DX​=∂vecT(X)∂f​=[∂X11​∂​,⋯,∂Xm1​∂​,⋯,∂X1n​∂​,⋯,∂Xmn​∂​]

  • 保持矩阵形式

DX=∂∂XT=(∂∂X11∂∂X21⋯∂∂Xm1∂∂X12∂∂X22⋯∂∂Xm2⋮⋮⋱⋮∂∂X1n∂∂X2n⋯∂∂Xmn)∈Rn×mD_{\mathbf{X}}=\frac{\partial }{\partial X^{T}}=\begin{pmatrix} \frac{\partial}{\partial X_{11}} & \frac{\partial}{\partial X_{21}} & \cdots & \frac{\partial}{\partial X_{m 1}} \\ \frac{\partial}{\partial X_{12}} & \frac{\partial}{\partial X_{22}} & \cdots & \frac{\partial}{\partial X_{m 2}} \\ \vdots & \vdots & \ddots & \vdots \\ \frac{\partial}{\partial X_{1 n}} & \frac{\partial}{\partial X_{2 n }} & \cdots & \frac{\partial}{\partial X_{mn}} \end{pmatrix} \in R^{n\times m} DX​=∂XT∂​=⎝⎜⎜⎜⎛​∂X11​∂​∂X12​∂​⋮∂X1n​∂​​∂X21​∂​∂X22​∂​⋮∂X2n​∂​​⋯⋯⋱⋯​∂Xm1​∂​∂Xm2​∂​⋮∂Xmn​∂​​⎠⎟⎟⎟⎞​∈Rn×m

事实上，两者有如下联系：

DvecX=rvec(DX)D_{vec X}=rvec(D_{X}) DvecX​=rvec(DX​)

矩阵函数的 Jacobian 矩阵

DXF(X)=def∂vec(F(X))∂(vecX)T∈Rpq×mnD_{X}F(X) \overset{\underset{\mathrm{def}}{}}{=} \frac{\partial vec(F(X))}{\partial (vec X)^{T}} \in R^{pq\times mn} DX​F(X)=def​∂(vecX)T∂vec(F(X))​∈Rpq×mn

梯度矩阵

与 Jacobian 矩阵的不同：

  • Jacobian: 分母是行向量
  • 梯度矩阵： 分母是列向量

关于向量：

∇x=∂∂x=[∂∂x1,∂∂x2,⋯ ,∂∂xm]T\nabla _{x}=\frac{\partial }{\partial x}=\left[ \frac{\partial }{\partial x_1 }, \frac{\partial }{\partial x_2},\cdots ,\frac{\partial }{\partial x_{m}} \right] ^{T} ∇x​=∂x∂​=[∂x1​∂​,∂x2​∂​,⋯,∂xm​∂​]T

关于矩阵：

∇X=(∂∂X11∂∂X12⋯∂∂X1n∂∂X21∂∂X22⋯∂∂X2n⋮⋮⋱⋮∂∂Xm1∂∂Xm2⋯∂∂Xmn)=∂∂X\nabla _{\mathbf{X}}=\begin{pmatrix} \frac{\partial}{\partial X_{11}} & \frac{\partial}{\partial X_{12}} & \cdots & \frac{\partial}{\partial X_{1n}} \\ \frac{\partial}{\partial X_{21}} & \frac{\partial}{\partial X_{22}} & \cdots & \frac{\partial}{\partial X_{2n}} \\ \vdots & \vdots & \ddots & \vdots \\ \frac{\partial}{\partial X_{m1}} & \frac{\partial}{\partial X_{m2}} & \cdots & \frac{\partial}{\partial X_{mn}} \end{pmatrix}=\frac{\partial }{\partial X} ∇X​=⎝⎜⎜⎜⎛​∂X11​∂​∂X21​∂​⋮∂Xm1​∂​​∂X12​∂​∂X22​∂​⋮∂Xm2​∂​​⋯⋯⋱⋯​∂X1n​∂​∂X2n​∂​⋮∂Xmn​∂​​⎠⎟⎟⎟⎞​=∂X∂​

可以看出：

  • Jacobian: 与分母的顺序是倒置的
  • 梯度矩阵： 与分母的顺序是一样的

二者的关系

∇X=DXT\nabla _{\mathbf{X}}= D_{\mathbf{X}}^{T} ∇X​=DXT​
