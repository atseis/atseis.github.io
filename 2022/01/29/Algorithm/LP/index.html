Primal, Dual 形式

Primal:

min⁡f0(x)s.t.fi(x)⩽0hi(x)=0\begin{aligned} \min &\quad f_0(x)\\ \text{s.t.} &\quad f_{i}(x)\leqslant 0\\ &\quad h_i(x) = 0\\ \end{aligned} mins.t.​f0​(x)fi​(x)⩽0hi​(x)=0​

Dual:

max⁡g(λ,ν)s.t.λ⩾0ν⩾0\begin{aligned} \max &\quad g(\lambda, \nu)\\ \text{s.t.} &\quad \lambda\geqslant 0\\ &\quad \nu\geqslant 0\\ \end{aligned} maxs.t.​g(λ,ν)λ⩾0ν⩾0​

g(λ,ν)=inf⁡x∈DL(x,λ,ν)=inf⁡x∈D[f0(x)+∑λifi(x)+∑νihi(x)]\begin{aligned} g(\lambda, \nu)&=\inf_{x \in D}L(x, \lambda, \nu) \\ &=\inf_{x \in D}\left[ f_0(x)+\sum_{}^{ } \lambda_{i}f_{i}(x)+\sum_{ }^{ } \nu_{i}h_{i}(x) \right] \end{aligned} g(λ,ν)​=x∈Dinf​L(x,λ,ν)=x∈Dinf​[f0​(x)+∑​λi​fi​(x)+∑​νi​hi​(x)]​

改变记法

若记：

t=f0(x)u=[f1(x),f2(x),⋯ ,fm(x)]T\begin{aligned} t&=f_0(x) \\ u&=[f_1(x), f_2(x), \cdots, f_m(x) ]^{T} \end{aligned} tu​=f0​(x)=[f1​(x),f2​(x),⋯,fm​(x)]T​

令：

G1={(t,u)∣x∈D}G2={(t,u)∣x∈R}\begin{aligned} G_1=\{(t,u)| x \in D\} \\ G_2=\{(t,u)| x \in \mathbb{R}\} \end{aligned} G1​={(t,u)∣x∈D}G2​={(t,u)∣x∈R}​
